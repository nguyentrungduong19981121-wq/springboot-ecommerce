    • GET/POST/PUT/DELETE /api/categories

    Features: Hierarchical structure (unlimited depth), cascade delete

    • GET/PUT /api/customers/profile

    Features: JWT authentication, BCrypt passwords, multiple addresses
    
5.  ✅ ORDER MANAGEMENT
    • GET/POST /api/orders
    • GET /api/orders/{id}
    • PUT /api/orders/{id}/status
    Features: Status workflow, auto stock reduction, filtering
    
6.  ✅ INVENTORY MANAGEMENT
    • GET /api/inventory
    • GET/PUT /api/inventory/{productId}
    Features: Import/export, transaction history (5 types)
    
7.  ✅ CHECKOUT PROCESS
    • POST /api/checkout
    • POST /api/checkout/apply-coupon
